How to Extract Video Frames

To use this tool, you need to extract frames from your video first. Here's how to do it:

Option 1: Using FFmpeg (Recommended)

If you have FFmpeg installed:

  1. Place your video at static/video.mp4
  2. Run the following command in your terminal:
    3. mkdir -p static/frames
ffmpeg -i static/video.mp4 -r 1 static/frames/frame_%04d.jpg
  3. This will extract 1 frame per second. If you want a different rate, change the -r 1 parameter.

Option 2: Use another extraction tool

Extract frames from your video and place them in the static/frames/ directory.

Refresh the page after extracting frames.
